About Ledger Trade & Capital

A trusted partner providing debt solutions to home builders and SFR investors through strategic programs with institutional capital.

Our Origin

Closing the Gap

The Ledger team shares a wealth of experience within residential housing, previously working at some of the largest SFR operators, aggregators, and investors in the world.

While navigating the institutional landscape, we noticed a disconnect between the products and tools employed by large-scale operators and what was available to local markets. We saw this as an opportunity to use our relationships and experience to bring institutional-grade products to the local level.

Ledger is built to offer the most efficient debt products available in residential housing, enabling local operators and home builders to grow their business effectively.

Our Approach

How We Work

We're a small, focused team. Four partners who serve as the sales team, backed by operations support. This means when you call, you talk to a decision-maker. No layers, no handoffs, no waiting.

Our capital comes through strategic relationships with institutional partners. This gives us the reliability and scale of a large lender with the responsiveness and flexibility of a small one.

We underwrite the deal, not just the borrower. If the numbers make sense and you can execute, we want to fund it.

What We Offer

Our Loan Products

  • Ground-Up Construction: Capital for builders to take projects from dirt to done
  • DSCR Loans: Long-term financing based on property cash flow, not personal income
  • Bridge Loans: Short-term capital for speed and flexibility between transactions
  • Fix & Flip: Fast funding to acquire, renovate, and sell

All products available in the $500K to $3M range.
