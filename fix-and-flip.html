Fix & Flip Loans

Fast funding to acquire, renovate, and sell. Designed for experienced operators who need reliable capital to close quickly.

Overview

Acquire, Renovate, Profit

Fix and flip loans provide short-term financing to purchase and renovate residential properties for resale. We fund the acquisition and the rehab, so you can focus on executing your renovation plan and getting to market.

Our draw process is fast and predictable. Submit your draw request, we inspect, you get funded. No delays, no surprises.

Loan Range

$500,000 to $3,000,000

Term

6 to 12 months with extension options

Funding

Acquisition + renovation costs

Closing Speed

As fast as 7 to 10 business days
